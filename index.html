Projects

The Mansion

Luxury. One word kept in mind throughout the design stage of this ultimate six bedrooms, 7.5 bathrooms mansion. The villa features floor to ceiling windows to provide as much natural light as possible. It comes with an arcade center, theater, a glass room for night gazing, bar, study, library, and pool.

Gaming Cave

This is the ultimate gaming cave design concept. It is design with the top of the line gaming computer and peripherals in mind. The gaming station houses ultra-wide monitors to VR headset for immersive experience, and if you ever get tired of digital pixels then blow off some steam on the pinball machine.

Design Studio

This is every designer's dream studio. It is designed with a workstation grade computer setup, 3D printing station, and a drafting station with its own computer setup.

Living Room

This living room is designed with the closest people around you in mind. It is place where you and you friends can hangout, watch movies, or play some video games and have fun.

232 Seigel Hotel

A Brooklyn styled hotel lobby and restaurant/ bar space designed for 232 Seigel Hotel, Brooklyn, New York, in partnership with Gensler and Heritage Equity Partners.

Brooklyn Queens Connector

This light rail station concept was designed for a Brooklyn Queens Connector which is a light rail project that would run from Queens to Brooklyn along the water front in New York. The design is inspired by industrial areas of Queens and Brooklyn and it is made to seem that it is a part of the streets.

Smallpox Hospital Repurposing

The Smallpox Hospital in Rooselvelt Island, New York has been abandoned since the 1950s, but now it is time to repurpose it. My High School partnered with a preservation firm to repurpose it and my idea was to turn into a community area and an observatory. I added another floor which will act as an observatory and an exhibit center. The intend is to give life to a place where many people met an unfortunate end.
